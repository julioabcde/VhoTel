FACILITIES

1. Spa

Our spa facilities also include relaxation lounges, where you can unwind before or after your treatments, as well as a fitness center and swimming pool to further enhance your wellness experience. We use only the finest quality products in our treatments, selected for their therapeutic properties and natural ingredients. Our team is dedicated to providing you with exceptional service, ensuring that your spa experience is tailored to your individual needs and preferences. At our spa hotel, we believe that self-care is essential for overall well-being. Indulge in our luxurious spa services and let us take care of you, leaving you feeling relaxed, rejuvenated, and ready to take on the world.

2. Gym

Our gym is equipped with a wide range of modern exercise equipment, carefully selected to meet the needs of our guests. From cardiovascular machines such as treadmills, stationary bikes, and elliptical trainers to strength training equipment including free weights, weight machines, and resistance bands, we have everything you need to maintain your fitness routine. Whether you prefer a high-intensity cardio session, a targeted strength training workout, or a combination of both, our gym offers a variety of options to suit your preferences. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are available to assist you with any equipment usage or provide guidance on proper exercise techniques. To enhance your workout experience, we offer amenities such as towels, water stations, and locker rooms with showers and changing facilities. You can conveniently freshen up after your workout before continuing with your day.

3. Swimming Pool

Our swimming pool services are designed to provide you with a refreshing and rejuvenating experience, whether you're seeking a leisurely swim or a fun-filled aquatic adventure. Our pool area is meticulously designed to create a serene and inviting atmosphere. Surrounded by lush landscaping and comfortable lounge chairs, it offers the perfect setting for sunbathing, reading a book, or simply soaking up the tranquil ambiance. The centerpiece of our pool area is our sparkling swimming pool, which is meticulously maintained to ensure cleanliness and safety. The pool is spacious and inviting, catering to both recreational swimmers and those looking for a refreshing dip. Dive into the cool water and feel the stress of the day melt away as you glide through the crystal-clear pool.
